What Can I do to help!

Support local teams

If you want to help football in ireland it is quiet easy and the simplest way to do that is just by supporting a team in ireland. The vast majority of irish football fans support foreign teams such as those in scotland, england and other clubs in the top 5 leagues of eurpoe. Supporting these clubs can be much cheaper compared to many who fly out of the country to attend matches. Supporting a local (or irish if no club is in your area) Team is the best way to suppport football in ireland

Volunteer

Clubs all across ireland are always looking for volunteers to help out from underage boys and girls teams all the way to the premier division clubs rely on volunteers to ensure smooth operations and the work is always there to be done from volunteer coaching to simply cleaning up after a game there are all manner of ways for you to help out your local club by volunteering. you can find out all about it by going to Volunteer Here!

Petition Government

Football like most sports is subsidised by the government who pay the fai who then split this amoung all avenuses of the game. the government also runs and owns many facilitie such as pitches, training facilities and stadiums in where football is played by voting for ploiticians who would push for enhancing irish football or demanding your current representative you can help by gettting football some extra recognition or help your local facilities so that football in ireland can continue and grow stronger!
