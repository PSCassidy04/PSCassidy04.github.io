The Latest News on the Irish Third Tier

Its Happening!

Its Finally Happening. On the 12th of December 2024 the FAi have announced the creation of the national League of Ireland. The New national league will stand as the third tier in the irish football pyramid and will act as a connecting point between the LOI and grassroots of irish football the League format will consist of 20 teams seperated into a north and south league of 10 teams each. This new league is set to begin in 2027 after the shorter league that is to follow from 2026 due to the aligned calander program that was passed earlier. Althought the 20 teams that will be entered are unknown they can begin the process to aply now and must do so before feburary 2025 in order to ensure their place. Thi is incredinle new as for years irish football fans have been crying out for a new division and after many years and passed deadlines or delays we can now go forward knowing that irish football is begining to go further in a positive direction.

Alliend Calanders

Great news for irish football as representitives from the FAI general assembley voted to introduce the Aligned Football Calander for Ireland. What this would mean for irish football is that all the regional leagues such as the LSL (Leinster Senior League) and MSL (Munster Senior League) will change their league season and fixtures to run in conjunction with the league of ireland as Ireland is a summer league while other leagues in ireland run as standered, this will lead to better condition of pitches across ireland and with league timetables inline will hopefully lead to a proper footballing pyramid in ireland. This Meeting is the first step in a proposed 11 point pathways plan for irish football and is a great sign of change for footballing in ireland if you would like to learn more you can find the original article here if you want to continue to support and help grow football in ireland check out our page where you can find how you can support irish football!
